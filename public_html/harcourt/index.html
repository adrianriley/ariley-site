Harcourt Software Limited is a software development consultancy company, formed in 1997 and based in Sheffield, UK.

Director and Consultant Adrian Riley is available for contract work. He is a highly experienced developer, having worked in IT for over 30 years. He has been involved mainly in Java development over the past several years, but is also familiar with several other languages, including Fortran, C and C++. He has also undertaken team leadership, project management, departmental management, training and mentoring.
